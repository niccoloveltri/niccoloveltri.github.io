A GSOS for Attribute-based Communication

Marino Miculan

University of Udine



Abstract: We propose a new GSOS semantics for AbC, a calculus for attribute-based communication. This semantics is simpler than the previous ones, but as much as expressive; in fact, we will show that there is a precise equivalence. The new semantics will be useful for simplifying bisimulation proofs, and it is preparatory to the definition of a bialgebraic semantics for AbC.

This is joint work with Matteo Paier.
